ACME Corporation Password Policy v2.0

1. PURPOSE
This document establishes the password policy for all ACME Corporation information systems and applications.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party users with access to ACME Corporation's information systems.

3. PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Minimum Requirements
- Length: 12 characters minimum
- Complexity: Must contain uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- No dictionary words or common phrases
- No personal information (names, birthdates, etc.)

3.2 Password Composition
- At least 2 uppercase letters
- At least 2 lowercase letters
- At least 2 numbers
- At least 2 special characters (!@#$%^&*)

3.3 Password Expiration
- Standard users: Every 90 days
- Administrators: Every 60 days
- Service accounts: Every 180 days

3.4 Password History
- Users cannot reuse their last 8 passwords
- Password history maintained for 2 years

4. ACCOUNT LOCKOUT POLICY
4.1 Failed Attempts
- 5 failed attempts triggers 30-minute lockout
- 10 failed attempts triggers 4-hour lockout
- 15 failed attempts requires administrator reset

4.2 Lockout Duration
- First lockout: 30 minutes
- Second lockout: 2 hours
- Third lockout: 4 hours
- Subsequent lockouts: Administrator intervention required

5. ADMINISTRATIVE ACCESS
5.1 Privileged Accounts
- All administrator access must be logged
- Separate admin account required for privileged operations
- Admin accounts reviewed quarterly

5.2 Remote Access
- VPN required for remote administrative access
- Multi-factor authentication mandatory
- Session timeout: 15 minutes inactivity

6. COMPLIANCE
This policy is reviewed annually and complies with:
- ISO 27001 Information Security Management
- SOC 2 Type II requirements
- Industry best practices

7. ENFORCEMENT
Violation of this policy may result in:
- Disciplinary action
- Access revocation
- Termination of employment
